Sourdough cheese and tomato pizza

Description

A thick crust of sourdough covered with a thin layer of Italian slow cooked herby tomato sauce and oozing with golden, tangy mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • Ready to roll out sourdough dough
  • Passata
  • Garlic
  • Herbs of choice
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Any other optional toppings of choice.

Steps

  1. Simmer the mozzarella, garlic and herbs for at least 20 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  3. Roll out the dough to your desired pizza size.
  4. When the sauce is ready, spread a thin layer onto the dough.
  5. Slice the mozzarella and place on top of the sauce.
  6. Bake the pizza for 20 minutes or until the dough has risen and the cheese is golden.
  7. Slice and serve!
