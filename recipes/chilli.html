Mixed bean chilli

Description

Mixed beans and vegetables in a spicy, tangy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tins of beans of your choice
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 red peppers
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 Tbsp chilli powder

Steps

  1. Fry the onions until softened, add the garlic and chilli powder and fry for a further minute.
  2. Add the peppers and carrots to the pan and fry until softened.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and tomato paste and simmer for at least 20 minutes.
  4. Serve over white rice and/or roasted sweet potatoes.
