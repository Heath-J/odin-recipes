Banana bread

Description

A deliciously moist and satisfying banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium ripe bananas
  • 250 g self-raising flour
  • 100 g sugar
  • 2 cups / 500 ml liquid (water or milk of your choice)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A handful of chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C and grease a loaf pan.
  2. Mash the bananas.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients together then stir in the mashed bananas, about half the liquid and any other ingredients you're using.
  4. Add more liquid as needed to form a thick cake batter texture.
  5. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and smooth the top of the loaf with a wet knife.
  6. Bake for at least 40 minutes or until the loaf is golden brown and coming away from the sides of the pan.
  7. Allow to cool thorougly before slicing and serving with a cup of tea!
